Course Outline:

  1. Introduction, Basic quantities, Ohm, Kirchhoff, Single-loop, Single Node-pair circuits
  2. Series-Parallel combinations of Resistors, Dependent Sources
  3. Nodal Analysis, Loop Analysis
  4. Network Theorems: Linearity, Superposition, Source transformation, Thevenin, Norton
  5. Energy storage elements, Capacitors, Inductors
  6. 1st and 2nd order circuits, Transient response
  7. AC steady-state analysis, Phasors, Impedance
  8. Transfer function, Bode plots, passive filters
  9. Laplace transforms and applications
