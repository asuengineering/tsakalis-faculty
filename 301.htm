Course Outline:

  1. Introduction, Motivating applications
  2. Basic quantities, Ohm, Kirchhoff, Single-loop, Single Node-pair circuits
  3. Series-Parallel combinations of Resistors, Dependent Sources, Measurements
  4. Nodal Analysis, Loop Analysis
  5. Op Amps
  6. Linearity, Superposition, Source transformation, Thevenin, Norton
  7. Energy storage elements, Capacitors, Inductors
  8. 1st and 2nd order circuits, Transient response
  9. AC steady-state analysis, Phasors, Impedance
